📘 About This App



Stock Market Portfolio is a simple web application that lets you manage and track your stock investments. With this app, you can:

  • ✅ Save stock ticker symbols to your personal portfolio
  • ➕ Add or ❌ delete stocks from your portfolio anytime
  • 🔍 Search for stock data using ticker symbols
  • 📊 Track stock prices with real-time market data
  • 📅 View daily open, high, low, close prices and volume
  • 📈 Check dividends and stock splits for historical analysis
